Design Work

@bostonu_ssa

Managed all social content, event graphics, and content calendars — growing engagement ~25% through consistent, community-driven storytelling across the full 2025–2026 academic year.

Across the Sea

Designed using a retro board game concept — illustrated food tokens, mixed-case typography, and a "collect as you go" mechanic that reflected the multicultural fair format. Brought together 4 SEA student orgs at BU.

Makan with BUSSA

Designed with a warm orange palette and hand-illustrated bento to evoke a shared meal far from home. Used "Makan" (to eat, in Malay/Indonesian) as the cultural anchor — instantly legible to the community.

Splash 2.0

Designed as a retro browser window UI — a playful nod to early internet aesthetics. The 3D-inflated "Splash" wordmark sits as the hero graphic over monochrome photo collages of past BUSSA events.

Lunar New Year FGM

Built around a traditional Chinese almanac — "12" as the bold date centrepiece, with playful 宜 SUIT / 忌 AVOID callouts lifted from the format. Tied the first general meeting to a cultural celebration.

LNY FGM — Flat Lay

Styled and shot a flat-lay version of the FGM poster for feed variety — cultural props and scene-setting to give the design a tangible, real-world presence on Instagram.

Great Singapore Social

Designed with a painterly aesthetic — bold all-caps type over an oil-painting-style kopitiam backdrop, referencing Singapore's hawker culture. Entry ticket sticker treatment adds a physical, tactile feel.
