Design Work

@usyd.suns

Grew the SUNS Instagram to 1,000+ followers over two years as Marketing Officer — building a content strategy that increased engagement ~30% through event-led campaigns, cross-club collabs, and consistent visual identity.

O-Week Welcome

Recruitment poster for Orientation Week — illustrated alphabet soup bowl with the tagline "We Just Need Nutrition Society." Warm, approachable, and immediately legible as food-culture branding.

Let's Go Bowling

Event poster for a members' social at Strike Bowling, Darling Harbour. Bold yellow with a retro sports-diner aesthetic — high energy, easy to read, designed to drive sign-ups.

Let's Do Some Yoga

Event poster for a SUNS × USU wellness session at Teachers College. Illustrated YOGA typography with illustrated figures — earthy, wellness-forward design that extended the SUNS brand beyond nutrition content.

From Barbell to Plate

Co-designed with BarbellSoc for a joint event on meal planning for lifters. Illustrated athlete, bold orange type, clean cream background — bridged both clubs' audiences through a shared visual identity.

From Barbell to Plate — Detail

Secondary graphic for the collab — minimalist barbell illustration with circular text highlighting three event pillars: roast meal plans, intro to weightlifting, and access to a professional dietitian.

AGM '23

"GM '23" rendered as an editorial lockup against a warm sunburst — opening post in a recruitment campaign to fill exec positions and drive Zoom attendance for the Annual General Meeting.
